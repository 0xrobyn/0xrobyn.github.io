my worldview

infrastructure is power made physical. i see the world as one big interconnected network - airlines, trainlines, finance, the internet. it's all systems woven together, written in different languages whether that's money, python, or protocols. understanding these systems isn't just technical curiosity - it's about seeing who has access and who doesn't. logistics is politics. every barrier is a choice someone made about who gets in and who stays out. consider your threat models and attack surfaces carefully.

i love figuring out ways to do what i love and get into spaces i want to be in. that's why i work in aviation (flight benefits = freedom of movement + seeing how the network actually functions), work festivals (because i LOVE concerts), and am learning event production. networking is my superpower. i've social-engineered my way into some wild situations (ate lunch with run the jewels, got invited to a private travis scott show, almost got hired by delta, contributed towards kanye's 10th album tracklist - stories for another time).
